About Me

Hello, I am Anindita Ghosh. I am from Kolkata, India. Currently I work as a researcher in the Agents and Simulated Reality group at the German Research Centre for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI), Saarbrúcken. I am working on deep-learning based human motion modelling to simulate motions of industrial workers. My research interests include Human motion synthesis, Machine learning in Vision and Graphics , and Motion Synthesis in Virtual/Augmented Reality . In my free time I like to bake cupcakes, read novels, and travel to new places.
