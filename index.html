About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Saarland University associated with the Visual Computing and Artificial Intelligence department at the Max-Planck-Institute for Informatics.

I work at the intersection of Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, and Multimodal Learning, developing generative models for human-centric content.

My research experience spans 3D synthesis, including human-human and human-scene interactions, spatiotemporal perception and generative world modeling, video and temporal synthesis, and multimodal understanding.

When I am not coding or building models, you will find me exploring the latest trends in AI 🤖, making delicious desserts 🍰, or traveling to new places ✈️.
